How to Use:

Click Browse to select Credit Card and/or Bank Statement files.

Multiple files (PDF/CSV/XLS) can be selected together.

Click "Extract & Save" to process.

If statements are password protected, ensure the correct passwords are saved in secrets.json.

Output Location:

Excel: Documents/cc-analysis/consolidated_statements.xlsx

Bank CSV: Documents/cc-analysis/consolidated_transactions.csv

Database: Documents/cc-analysis/*.db

Errors: AppData/cc-analysis/error.log
